The Itemized Needs Marketplace: How It Works

Since we are just launching, we are focused on recruiting our first cohort of non-profit partners. This marketplace will soon host tangible needs, ensuring every dollar is accountable.

1. Itemized Needs

Charities post specific items like **"Three new laptops for student tutors"** or **"$200 for event permit fees."** This eliminates ambiguity for donors.

2. Impact Passport

Donors receive a digital tracker and **photo proof** when the funded item is purchased and delivered. **Trust** is built through radical, real-time accountability.

3. Talent Match

Charities also post needs for **pro-bono student skills** (e.g., social media strategy, flyer design), linking them to our pool of skilled youth volunteers.
